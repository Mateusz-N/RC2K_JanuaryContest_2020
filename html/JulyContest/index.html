Overview

New July Contest Test is a Rally Championship 2000 contest, where your goal is to conquer all 6 rallies in the BRC Championship mode, which features legendary cars from classes A5, A6 and A7, and do so the faster than your opponents. This event marks the 4th anniversary of the modern-day RC2K community, hence the name. Fasten your seatbelts and get ready to rumble!

The rivalry will be made as fair as possible by assessing each player's skill and assigning each of them an appropriate set of cars to choose from. For example, a tenured pro will only get to pick an A5 car, while a Sunday driver will be offered a vehicle from the A7 class.

The contest will last from January 14, 2020 to January 28, 2020 and there will be no strict schedule, ie, the championship may be completed at any point during these 2 weeks.

Validation process

Every participant must take two screenshots after each stage: a screenshot that shows the stage time and a screenshot that shows the overall rally time (both screens can be accessed after completing a stage, just take a screenshot of them). Additionally, it's advised to record your runs, or at least send the replay files, so the Championship Manager (me) can ensure no rule was broken. The screenshots and the videos/replays must be sent to me no later than at 23:59 UTC+1, January 28, 2020. It's best to send them via DM on Discord, but other means are also accepted, as long as the material finds its way to me.

Rules

  • No cheats or modifications affecting the gameplay mechanics are allowed;
  • The entire championship has to be completed without any retries, even if you don't finish a rally (in which case you're ranked last in the final ranking of the rally you didn't finish and you must move onto the next one);
  • Taking any of these shortcuts is prohibited and will be punished (i.e., please drive as cleanly as possible);
  • You must stick to your car until the very end;
  • UI, camera view and other game settings may be freely changed;
  • Service area car setups and the transmission mode are up to you to decide;

Closing words

I hope all of you have a great time and perform at your peak! If you have any questions, ask away in the Discord server. Good luck & have fun!
